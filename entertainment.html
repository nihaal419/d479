Entertainment

Beaches & Rainforest Adventures

Most people visit Taniti to enjoy the beautiful beaches, explore the lush rainforest, and visit the island's active volcano. Whether you want to relax on white, sandy shores or hike through tropical greenery, Taniti offers unforgettable natural experiences for every traveler.

Exciting Activities

Beyond the beaches, Taniti offers a variety of activities: visit a local history museum, go on chartered fishing tours, snorkel in crystal-clear waters, or zip-line through the rainforest. There's something for every adventurer!

Nightlife & Entertainment

Enjoy Taniti's vibrant nightlife: visit several pubs, including a microbrewery, dance at the new club, see a movie, or try your luck at the arcade. Art galleries and bowling alleys offer fun for all ages, and a nine-hole golf course is coming soon!

Merriton Landing

Many of Taniti's most popular activities are located in Merriton Landing, a rapidly developing area on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay. Discover new attractions, entertainment venues, and local culture in this lively district.

Sightseeing & Tours

Most tourists spend their time in Taniti City, known for its native architecture and proximity to the beaches of Yellow Leaf Bay. Popular sightseeing options include boat or bus tours, rainforest hikes, and volcano visits for breathtaking views.
